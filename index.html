Games built by Britt Poplin (aka ylempoint at ylem's ink) do not store or transmit your personal data. The games do store high score and game save information in the user preferences of the device that the game is loaded onto.

NOTE2: The free version of the games (not the paid versions) do use Unity Ads and Google AdMob to serve ads. Stack and Bomb Free may allow third parties to collect Non-personal data through their own embedded technology in order to deliver ads to you via a unique cookie or similar technology for non-personally identifiable information.
